Task 2 - Planning and Designing the Solution

This task focuses on:

  • Planning the technical solution to meet the requirements identified in Task 1.
  • Designing the user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) of the solution.
  • Creating wireframes, prototypes, and flowcharts to map out the system's structure and functionality.

Deliverables for this task might include:

  • Wireframes and mockups of the solution.
  • A detailed project plan with milestones and timelines.
  • Documented technical specifications and any chosen technologies.
