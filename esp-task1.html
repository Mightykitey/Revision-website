Task 1 - Understanding the Requirements

In this task, you will need to:

  • Understand the project brief and the problem that needs solving.
  • Research the project requirements, such as technical specifications and user needs.
  • Identify stakeholders and gather initial requirements for the solution.

Deliverables for this task might include:

  • A detailed report outlining the requirements.
  • Identification of key features and constraints.
